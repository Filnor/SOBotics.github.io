Heat Detector's FAQ

  1. Commenting back on reports is strictly prohibited. Users found leaving comments when acting on reports will be kicked from SOCVFinder chat room, and it is up to the RO's discretion to terminate the bot if it happens.
  2. It is not allowed to speak about the users involved in SOCVFinder chat room. We do not moderate users, but comments.
  3. When multiple offensive comments are exchanged, flag the thread for moderator intervention and move on.
  4. tp should be used for "not nice" comments or comments that lead to more offensive ones. You can flag the comment approprietly.
  5. fp should be used for comments that were wrongly reported (i.e. they are on-topic and constructive).
